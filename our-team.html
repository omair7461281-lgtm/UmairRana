Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We Have Answers

Find answers to common questions about our services, development process, project timelines, technologies, support, and everything you need to know before starting your digital journey with us.

What types of web development services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive web development services including custom website development, full-stack applications, e-commerce solutions, CMS development, mobile app development, and ERP/CRM systems. Our expertise covers both frontend and backend technologies to deliver complete solutions.

How long does it take to complete a typical web project?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. A simple website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while complex web applications may take 2-6 months. We provide detailed project timelines during the planning phase and keep you updated throughout the development process.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive support and maintenance packages including regular updates, security patches, performance optimization, bug fixes, and 24/7 technical support. We ensure your website remains secure, up-to-date, and performing at its best.

What technologies do you work with?

We work with modern technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, Vue.js, Angular, Node.js, PHP, Python, .NET, MySQL, MongoDB, and various CMS platforms like WordPress and Shopify. We choose the best technology stack based on your project requirements.

How do you ensure website security?

We implement robust security measures including SSL certificates, secure coding practices, regular security audits, data encryption, protection against common vulnerabilities (XSS, SQL injection), and compliance with data protection regulations like GDPR.

Can you help with SEO and digital marketing?

Absolutely! We provide SEO optimization services including on-page SEO, technical SEO, content optimization, and performance improvements. We also offer digital marketing services to help improve your online visibility and drive traffic to your website.

What is your pricing structure?

Our pricing is project-based and depends on factors like complexity, features, timeline, and ongoing support needs. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your requirements. We offer flexible payment plans and transparent pricing with no hidden costs.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes, we work with clients globally. Our team is experienced in remote collaboration and we adapt to different time zones. We communicate effectively through various channels and ensure smooth project delivery regardless of location.
